Peachy

At Peachy, we wanted to combat this issue and find some sort of way increase access to healthcare and to ultimately encourage our age group to look after themselves and check up on their health. We’ve created an innovative 3-part service that allows customers to conduct blood tests in the convenience of their own home. Customers can keep track of their past results through our website, and will then receive their monthly ‘PeachyBox’ based off of their test results.

Versara

Versara marketplace platform for a secure peer-to-peer rental community for immediate needs. Our product allows for users to request and upload items that collect dust in their garages and closets to be recycled into the marketplace again.
