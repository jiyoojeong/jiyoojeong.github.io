About Jiyoo!

SOCIAL EQUITY-JUSTICE ADVOCATE * RELENTLESS CREATIVE * PROBLEMS ENGINEER

Jiyoo is a student at the University of California - Berkeley studying Business, Computer Science and Statistics.

She is deeply passionate about the intersectionalities of seemingly diverse fields like art, science, history, business and social justice. She hopes to continue with her creative passions into the future, engineering solutions to the world's wicked problems. Currently, Jiyoo is working on several independent projects related to social sector impact investment and entrepreneurship to help expand the existing paradigms of financial technology and investment to create purpose-driven organizations.
