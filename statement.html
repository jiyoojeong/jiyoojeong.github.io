Biography

co-founder of Versara, social justice advocate, and relentless creative.

Jiyoo is a rising second year student at the University of California - Berkeley studying Computer Science and Statistics. She is deeply passionate about the intersectionalities of seemingly diverse fields like art, science, business and social justice. With deep roots in art, she hopes to continue with her creative passions into the future, engineering solutions to the world's wicked problems.

Currently, she is working as a Campus Ambassador for the university as well as the Program Assistant for the American Cultures Center. Jiyoo has interned for startups in the Berkeley Skydeck Cohort program, serving as a campus manager for CatalystX and a business development intern at Artivive. In the summer of 2019, Jiyoo is broadening her scope of experiences by delving into nanotechnology research as an undergraduate assistant and creating a new company, Versara, along side her three co-founders.

Jiyoo spends her evenings working on projects and artworks. Her focus is on the essence of light and reflection. She is also learning how to create websites and mobile apps. In her free time, Jiyoo likes to visit the local thrift stores of Berkeley to find hidden gems others have donated. She also enjoys picnicking on the school glades and listening to podcasts on the future of design, technology and society.
